Why Sign Up?

By signing up with you name and email, you can recieve weekly email notifications about the SKYCTC Game Club including meeting times and location, reminders for upcoming events, opportunities to vote on future activites, and more! We promise not to send more than 2 emails in any given week, and if you ever want to unsubscribe, all you have to do is reply "Stop" to any email from us! We look forward to seeing you at the next meeting!
